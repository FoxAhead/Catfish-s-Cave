Terrain

If there's one thing I couldn't stand about Test of Time, it was the default graphics. They are actually worse than the 8-bit graphics of the original game! The most obvious graphics in the game are the terrains and the default ones had to go! Here, I have an assortment of 24-bit terrains for Test of Time (which, by the way, renders its graphics in 16-bit colour - although this is effectively reduced to 15-bit, since 1 bit appears to be reserved for non-visible masks). Some terrains are made by me, some modified by me and some created by others (see the Readme files in the zips for details).

After reading this thread about  terrain tiling in Civ2 by Mercator over at Apolyton and then trying it out myself, I decided to update the bulk of my terrains to this format. It will effectively remove the slight overlap of adjacent tiles. The improvement is most noticeable for roads and railroads.
